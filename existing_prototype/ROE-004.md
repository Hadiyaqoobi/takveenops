## Context
The 8-dimension matching weights are currently based on academic research (Sackett et al. 2022).
We need to validate these weights against real refugee-employer matches to see if adjustments are needed.

## Dimensions to Validate
1. skills_match (0.27) — strongest signal
2. experience_relevance (0.20) — log-scaled
3. logistics_score (0.15) — commute/shift fit
4. retention_signal (0.15) — LightGBM prediction
5. language_capability (0.10) — English proficiency
6. education_fit (0.05) — weakest predictor
7. culture_fit (0.05) — P-O fit model
8. interview_readiness (0.03) — AQ/Grit assessment
